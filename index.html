About Us

Daughters of Niger Delta is a community-driven initiative that unites, empowers, and advocates for women in the Niger Delta through education, leadership, and environmental justice.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To build a thriving, inclusive, and empowered society where the voices, rights, and contributions of Niger Delta women are respected, celebrated, and integral to sustainable development.

Mission

To unite, empower, and advocate for the women of the Niger Delta by promoting education, economic independence, leadership, environmental justice, and social equity through community-driven initiatives, partnerships, and policy engagement.

Our Projects

Education Empowerment

Scholarships and training programs for girls and women.

Environmental Justice

Advocating for cleaner, safer communities in the Niger Delta.

Leadership Development

Mentorship and training for women leaders of tomorrow.
